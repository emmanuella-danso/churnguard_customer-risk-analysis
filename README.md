# 📊 ChurnGuard Telecom Customer Risk & Churn Analysis

## 🧠 Project Overview

**ChurnGuard** is a data-driven telecom analytics project designed to identify high-risk customers, quantify revenue exposure, and recommend targeted retention strategies.

The goal is to help telecom companies reduce customer churn by proactively identifying customers likely to disengage based on behavior, spending patterns, and risk scoring.

This project combines **Python (data engineering + feature creation)** and **Power BI (business intelligence dashboards)** to deliver actionable insights for decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Telecom companies lose significant revenue due to customer churn, often without early warning signals.

This project answers key business questions:

- Which customers are at risk of churn?
- How much revenue is at risk?
- What behavioral patterns indicate churn risk?
- What actions should be taken for each customer segment?

## 📈 Key Insights

- A small percentage of customers contribute disproportionately to revenue risk.
- High volatility in spending is a strong indicator of churn probability.
- “Urgent Action” customers require immediate retention intervention.
- Customer behavior patterns are more predictive than static demographics.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Prioritize retention efforts on **Urgent Action customers**
- Implement proactive engagement for **At Risk segment**
- Monitor high volatility spending patterns weekly
- Introduce early warning churn detection systems
